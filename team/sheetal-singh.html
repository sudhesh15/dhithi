Advisor

Ms. Sheetal has over 30 years of experience in Urban Development, Circular Economy, Rural Development, Water and Sanitation, Public Health, Hygiene and Nutrition, Renewable Energy, and Sustainable Development. She has worked across NGOs, MNCs, and government sectors, bringing a wealth of expertise in driving impactful initiatives. Her strong technical and administrative acumen, coupled with the ability to navigate complex regulatory landscapes, makes her a valuable asset to any organization.

Her strategic approach and dedication to excellence continue to contribute to transformative solutions in the sectors she serves.
